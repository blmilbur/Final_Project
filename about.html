Our Story

Capturing the Heartlands

Based in Lebanon, Indiana, Midwest Art endeavors to capture the essence of the United States heartlands through a camera lens. Our staff of Armed Forces veterans provide a collection of unique and interesting pieces that will fit in any home.

Making a Difference

Midwest Art has two simple goals.

1. Provide gainful and well-paying employment to our armed forces veterans.

2. Donate all profits to local veteran-oriented charities to improve our communities and the quality of life for those who have served our country.

Accordingly, Midwest Art has donated over $100,000 dollars to over 16 local veteran's charities. We're motivated and dedicated to make a difference.
